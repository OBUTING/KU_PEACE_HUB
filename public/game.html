A short game for peacebuilders

The land is split down the middle.

On one side, the highlands. On the other, the plains, the lake, the coast. Between them: a gorge where a bridge used to stand. It fell the last time people stopped listening to each other. You'll cross three places where trust has cracked — a classroom, a water line, a forest — and decide, choice by choice, whether the bridge gets rebuilt.
