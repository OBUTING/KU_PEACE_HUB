Kenya's real emergency & reporting contacts

Who to call, and when

Police / general emergency

999 · 112

For anything happening right now — violence, immediate danger, a crime in progress.

NCIC — hate speech & incitement

020 258 5702

WhatsApp: 0702 777 000
The National Cohesion and Integration Commission handles ethnic incitement, hate speech, and threats to national cohesion. cohesion.go.ke

KNCHR — human rights violations

0800 720 627 (toll-free)

SMS: 22359 · Email: complaint@knchr.org
The Kenya National Commission on Human Rights monitors and responds to rights violations, including excessive force.

Emotional / trauma support

0800 724 280 (free)

Trained counsellors, free of charge, if you or someone you know is shaken by something you witnessed or experienced.
